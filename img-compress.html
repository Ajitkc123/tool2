Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool completely free?

Yes, our image compression tool is 100% free to use with no hidden charges or registration required.

What image formats are supported?

Currently, we support JPEG, PNG, and WEBP formats for compression.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size for upload is 10MB per image.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. All compression happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

How does the quality slider work?

The quality slider allows you to balance between file size and image quality. Lower values result in smaller files with reduced quality, while higher values maintain better quality with larger file sizes.
